# JJTeam Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-05-09
**Last updated:** 2026-05-09

JJTeam (the "App") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy is published in accordance with Article 30 of the Korean Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) and aligns with the requirements of Apple's App Store and Google Play.

The App does not communicate with any external server. Any information you enter is stored locally on your device. The App operator therefore has no access to your data and never collects or transmits it.

---

## 1. Information We Store

The App stores the following information that you enter, locally on your device using `AsyncStorage`:

- Team name
- Member names and grades (high / mid / low)
- Court and queue configuration
- Time thresholds, court count, capacity per court, and other settings
- Operational data such as queue enqueue timestamps

This information never leaves your device and is not transmitted to the developer or any third party.

---

## 2. Purpose of Processing

The stored information is used solely to:

- Restore your previous state (team setup, member list, settings) when you reopen the App.

---

## 3. Retention Period

Information is retained on your device for as long as you use the App. It is permanently destroyed when you delete the data within the App or uninstall the App.

---

## 4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share your information with any third party. Because data is stored only on your device, there is no transmission path to any external party.

---

## 5. Outsourcing of Processing

We do not outsource the processing of your information to any third party.

---

## 6. Destruction Procedure and Method

- **Procedure:** When you delete information within the App or uninstall the App, the operating system's app-data removal mechanism destroys the data immediately.
- **Method:** Data is permanently deleted from local device storage. No backups or external copies exist.

You can erase all data at any time by:

1. Uninstalling the App, or
2. Going to your device Settings → Apps → JJTeam → Storage → Clear data.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Under Articles 35 to 37 of the Korean Personal Information Protection Act, you (as the data subject) have the right to access, correct, delete, and request the suspension of processing of your information.

Because all data is stored on your device, you can directly exercise these rights:

- **Access:** View your information directly on screen within the App.
- **Correction:** Edit member names, grades, and other settings via the Team Settings menu.
- **Deletion:** Delete individual members in Team Settings, or clear all data via the device app-data deletion.
- **Suspension:** Stop using or uninstall the App to immediately stop any processing.

For additional inquiries, contact the Privacy Officer (Section 12 below).

---

## 8. Security Measures

We protect your information through the following design choices:

- **No external transmission:** The App does not send your data over the internet, Bluetooth, or any other external channel.
- **Local storage only:** All data is stored only in the device's app-isolated storage (`AsyncStorage`), inaccessible to other apps.
- **No analytics, ads, or crash-reporting SDKs:** No tracking SDKs are bundled with the App.
- **Principle of least privilege:** The App requests no system permissions (no camera, location, microphone, contacts, etc.).

---

## 9. Automated Collection Tools (Cookies, etc.)

The App does not use cookies, identifiers, tracking pixels, or any other automated collection mechanism.

---

## 10. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 14 (or under 13 outside Korea) and does not knowingly collect data from them.

---

## 11. App Permissions

The App requests no system permissions — no camera, location, microphone, contacts, photos, notifications, or other device-level access.
