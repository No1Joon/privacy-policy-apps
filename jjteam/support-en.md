# JJTeam Support

For questions, bug reports, or feature requests regarding JJTeam, please reach out via the email below.

## Contact

- **Email:** [jjrottensweetpotato@gmail.com](mailto:jjrottensweetpotato@gmail.com)
- **Response time:** Within 1–3 business days

## Helpful info to include

- Device (e.g., iPhone 15, Galaxy S24)
- OS version (e.g., iOS 18.1, Android 15)
- App version
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Screenshots, if available

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q. Where is my data stored?**
A. All data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external server. See the [Privacy Policy](./privacy-en) for details.

**Q. What happens to my data if I delete the app?**
A. Deleting the app removes all JJTeam data stored on your device.
